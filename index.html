Important Disclaimer

Use of MTRT does not apply to:

  • STAT doses
  • First doses and loading doses
  • One-time doses
  • Specifically timed doses (e.g. antibiotics for pre-procedure, drug desensitization protocols)
  • Time-sequenced or concentrationcritical medications (e.g. chemotherapeutic, rescue agents)
  • Drugs administered at specific times to ensure accurate peak/trough/serum drug levels (e.g. vancomycin)
  • PPN medications
